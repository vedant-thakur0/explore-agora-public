4. **Stability under normalization**: Top Democratic tags (Bias, Security, Discrimination) remain dominant in both methods, though percentages shift (93.1% → 68.0% for Bias)

5. **Republican positioning**: Still lacks a counter-platform even under document normalization; input controls remain the only strong differentiator
